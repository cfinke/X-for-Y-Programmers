Twenty hands-on lessons that translate what you already know from Go into Python. Each lesson shows a familiar Go idiom, gives you a small problem to solve in Python, and checks your answer by running it on Wandbox (internet connection required). Your progress is saved locally, and a reference solution is one click away if you get stuck.
