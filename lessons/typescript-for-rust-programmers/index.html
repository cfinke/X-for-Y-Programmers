Twenty hands-on lessons that translate what you already know from Rust into TypeScript. Each lesson shows a familiar Rust idiom, gives you a small problem to solve in TypeScript, and checks your answer right in the browser — no toolchain required. Your progress is saved locally, and a reference solution is one click away if you get stuck.

    Rust ⇄ TypeScript cheat sheet

    A comprehensive quick reference pulled together from all 20 lessons — every idiom mapped between the two languages, in one place.
