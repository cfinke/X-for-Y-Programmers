Twenty hands-on lessons that translate what you already know from Rust into C++. Each lesson shows a familiar Rust idiom, gives you a small problem to solve in C++, and checks your answer by compiling and running it on Wandbox (internet connection required). Your progress is saved locally, and a reference solution is one click away if you get stuck.
